## Treatment standard

All program cards use `TreatedPhoto`:

- Dark lower gradient for text readability
- Radial vignette to conceal casual environments
- Subtle blue edge lighting
- Consistent aspect ratio
- Natural skin tones preserved (no blue skin cast)
